Duʿāʾs
How Can I?

Written after getting musalsal ḥadīth of shaking hands and interlocking hands

How can I wash my hand that has touched you ﷺ? But I do, raising my hands, asking that I see you ﷺ. My mouth that utters your sweet concise speech ﷺ? But I do, dreaming of the day I get to speak to you ﷺ. My nose that breathes the same air you breathed ﷺ? But I do, imagining the day I get to smell you ﷺ. My face that smiles at the mention of your blessed name ﷺ? But I do, gitty over the day I get to be kissed by you ﷺ. My arm that grazes by your perfect grave ﷺ? But I do, longing for the day I get to be hugged by you ﷺ. My head that can't stop thinking about you ﷺ? But I do, awaiting the day I will be thought about by you ﷺ. My ears that hear your gentle words and beautiful praises ﷺ? But I do, yearning for the day I get to hear you ﷺ. And my feet that tread the same earth you tread ﷺ? But I do, as I live my life running to meet you ﷺ.

يَا رَبِّي قُلْ لَهُ: يَا أَيَّتُهَا النَّفْسُ الْمُطْمَئِنَّةُ، ارْجِعِي إِلَىٰ رَبِّكِ رَاضِيَةً مَّرْضِيَّةً، فَادْخُلِي فِي عِبَادِي، وَادْخُلِي جَنَّتِي

Yā Rabbī, Qullahu [recite last 4 verses of Sūrah al-Fajr]

O My Lord, Say to him: "O you calm-tanquil soul! Return back to your Lord well-pleased and pleasing (to Allāh ﷻ). Be among My [righteous] slaves. Enter My Garden." [Al-Fajr 89:27-30]

رَبِّ أَرِهِ يَنْظُرْ إِلَيْكَ

Rabbi ʾarihi yanẓur ʾilayka

My Lord! Show Yourself to him, so he can see You.
