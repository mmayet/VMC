Duʿāʾs
يَا رَبِّي ارْضَ بِهِ

Yā Rabbī 'rḍa bihi

O My Lord, be pleased with him.

يَا رَبِّي قُلْ لَهُ: يَا أَيَّتُهَا النَّفْسُ الْمُطْمَئِنَّةُ، ارْجِعِي إِلَىٰ رَبِّكِ رَاضِيَةً مَّرْضِيَّةً، فَادْخُلِي فِي عِبَادِي، وَادْخُلِي جَنَّتِي

Yā Rabbī, Qullahu [recite last 4 verses of Sūrah al-Fajr]

O My Lord, Say to him: "O you calm-tanquil soul! Return back to your Lord well-pleased and pleasing (to Allāh ﷻ). Be among My [righteous] slaves. Enter My Garden." [Al-Fajr 89:27-30]

رَبِّ أَرِهِ يَنْظُرْ إِلَيْكَ

Rabbi ʾarihi yanẓur ʾilayka

My Lord! Show Yourself to him, so he can see You.
